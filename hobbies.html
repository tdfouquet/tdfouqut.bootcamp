One of my favorite hobbies besides playing video games is frisbee golfing!

Frisbee Golfing (frolfing for short), or disc golf, is a great way to experience the outdoors! It is golfing but with a frisbee instead. The rules are the same as golf, and instead of a hole it's a basket. A lot of the courses are in wooded area's for more difficulties.

It is a great way to relieve stress, and anger. Cause sometimes you just gotta throw something.
